Sweet Potato Cookies Recipe


Malawian mbatata cookies made with sweet potatoes are a healthier alternative to your everyday cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mashed sweet potato about 2 sweet potatoes see note in Instructions
  • 1 ¼ cup all purpose sifted flour
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 4 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ cup raisins

Steps

  1. Peel, cut up the potatoes into pieces and boil for about 20 minutes or until fork tender
  2. Smash the potatoes with fork or potato smacher
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  4. Mix sweet potatoes milk, egg, and melted butter in a bowl and beat together
  5. Stir in the remaining ingredients.
  6. Drop by the spoonful onto a greased cookie sheet and bake for 15 minutes
  7. Alternatively you can chill the dough for an hour and roll out on a floured surface
  8. Use a heart shaped cookie cutter and place on a greased baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 15 minutes
  10. You can garnish with some cinnamon and sugar if desired

    11. Enjoy your potato cookies!!
